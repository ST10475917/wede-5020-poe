Our Story

Supreme Cuts wasn’t just opened — it was built. In 2023, 2 barbers got tired of rushed cuts and bad service. We started in a small 2-chair shop with one rule: Every client leaves sharper than they came in.

Today we’ve cut 2000+ heads, built a loyal crew, and became the spot for clean fades in Cape Town. We don’t do “quick trims”. We do transformations.
